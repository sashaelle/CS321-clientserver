# Blackjack Client-Server Project
Blackjack is a common card game. The game uses a standard deck of 52 cards and is also known as "21". 

## How to play 
The goal of Blackjack is to approach the card combination value of 21. The player must get as close to that value without going over. Going over would result in a "Bust" and the player would lose the game. 

Cards numbered 2-10 hold the value of the card as shown. All face cards (Jack, Queen, King) are also valued at 10. An Ace is valued at 11 unless the player's other cards sum to be more than 21, then the Ace is valued at 1. 

Blackjack is played between players and a single dealer. Each player is dealt 2 cards at the start of the game. The dealer is also dealt 2 cards, but the dealer only presents one of the cards to the player. If the player's hand is valued at less than 21, then the player has an option to "Hit". This means they receive another card that from the top of the deck. The player is allowed to "Hit" as many times as they desire, so long they do not go over the value of 21. The player's other option is to "Hold". This means they are done asking for more cards and would like to see the value of the dealer's hand. 

The dealer holds special "Hit" and "Hold" rules. If the cards of the dealer's hand is less than 17, the dealer is required to hit until the value is 17 or greater. If the dealer goes over 21, the player would be declared winner of the game.
